The Crew

Ken: The Creator

His passion for everything nerdy started as a young kid and only grew as he got older. He’s an avid fan of Star Wars, Doctor Who, Harry Potter, Anime, Video Games, Prop Building... the list goes on and on. He grew up all over in a military family but considers Orlando his home. Ken went to UCF for his Management Information Systems degree and currently works in the IT field. When he’s not at home playing games with his best bud Ghost, the silver Labrador, he can usually be found around town at one event or another. He has a passion for cooking and learning new things and he also enjoys doing charity work. MS Society & American Cancer Society are just some of the charities he works hard to support. Ken started the show to express his creative side and was lucky enough to find la ike-minded co-hosts in Arthur.

Arthur: The Cool, Calm & Collected

He’s a self-proclaimed “casual nerd” who enjoys D&D, gaming, movies, Magic the Gathering, and fantasy football. He’s our in-house horror buff and lover of anything spooky. Arthur came on to the show after the 2017 Halloween episode and immediately fell in love with podcasting. When not recording, you can usually find him reading, working on fitness goals, drinking bourbon, or spending time with his lovely girlfriend, Rosanna and his pup, Coco.
